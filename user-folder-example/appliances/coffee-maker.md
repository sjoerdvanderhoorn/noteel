# Coffee Maker - Model CM-2000

## Product Information
**Brand:** BrewMaster  
**Model:** CM-2000  
**Purchase Date:** March 15, 2024  
**Warranty:** 2 years

## Features
- 12-cup capacity
- Programmable timer
- Auto shut-off after 2 hours
- Brew strength selector
- Reusable filter included
- Hot plate warmer

## Daily Use Instructions
1. Fill water reservoir to desired level
2. Add coffee grounds to filter basket (1-2 tbsp per cup)
3. Press BREW button or set timer for automatic brewing
4. Wait for brewing cycle to complete (approximately 10 minutes)
5. Pour and enjoy!

## Cleaning & Maintenance
- **Daily:** Rinse carafe and filter basket
- **Weekly:** Wash removable parts with warm soapy water
- **Monthly:** Run descaling cycle with vinegar solution
  - Fill reservoir with equal parts water and white vinegar
  - Run brew cycle without coffee
  - Run 2-3 cycles with clean water to rinse

## Troubleshooting
- **Not brewing:** Check that it's plugged in and water reservoir is filled
- **Weak coffee:** Increase amount of coffee grounds or use finer grind
- **Slow brewing:** Time to descale the machine
- **Overflowing:** Don't exceed max fill line on water reservoir
