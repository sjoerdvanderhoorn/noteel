# Microwave - Model MW-1200

## Product Information
**Brand:** QuickHeat  
**Model:** MW-1200  
**Purchase Date:** June 8, 2024  
**Warranty:** 1 year limited  
**Power:** 1200 watts

## Features
- 1.2 cubic feet capacity
- 10 power levels
- One-touch buttons for common items
- Sensor cooking technology
- Turntable (12.5")
- Child safety lock

## Quick Start Guide
1. Place food in microwave-safe container
2. Close door
3. Press time/power buttons or use preset
4. Press START
5. Wait for beep, carefully remove hot food

## Preset Buttons
- **Popcorn:** 2.5 - 3.5 minutes
- **Potato:** 4-6 minutes per potato
- **Pizza:** Reheats 1-3 slices
- **Beverage:** 1-2 cups
- **Frozen Dinner:** 4-8 minutes

## Power Level Guide
- **100% (High):** Water, beverages, soups
- **70% (Medium-High):** Cooking meats, reheating
- **50% (Medium):** Gentle cooking, simmering
- **30% (Low):** Defrosting, melting chocolate
- **10% (Warm):** Keeping food warm

## Safety Tips
- Never operate empty
- Use only microwave-safe containers
- No metal or aluminum foil
- Pierce foods with skins (potatoes, hot dogs)
- Stir liquids before and after heating
- Let food stand 1-2 minutes after cooking

## Cleaning
- Wipe interior after each use with damp cloth
- For stubborn stains: Heat bowl of water + lemon juice for 3 minutes, then wipe
- Clean turntable in sink with dish soap
- Wipe exterior with all-purpose cleaner

## Troubleshooting
- **Not heating:** Check door closes properly, verify power setting
- **Turntable not rotating:** Ensure it's properly seated on support
- **Sparking:** Remove any metal, check for damaged waveguide cover
- **Display not working:** Reset by unplugging for 30 seconds
