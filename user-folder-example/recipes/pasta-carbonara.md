# Pasta Carbonara

## Ingredients
- 400g spaghetti
- 200g pancetta or bacon, diced
- 4 large eggs
- 100g Parmesan cheese, grated
- Black pepper to taste
- Salt for pasta water

## Instructions
1. Bring a large pot of salted water to boil
2. Cook spaghetti according to package directions
3. While pasta cooks, fry pancetta in a large pan until crispy
4. In a bowl, whisk together eggs, Parmesan, and black pepper
5. Drain pasta, reserving 1 cup of pasta water
6. Add hot pasta to pan with pancetta
7. Remove from heat and quickly stir in egg mixture
8. Add pasta water a little at a time to create a creamy sauce
9. Serve immediately with extra Parmesan

**Total Time:** 20 minutes  
**Serves:** 4 people
