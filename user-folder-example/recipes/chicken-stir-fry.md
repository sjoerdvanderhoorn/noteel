# Chicken Stir-Fry

## Ingredients
- 500g chicken breast, sliced
- 2 tbsp vegetable oil
- 2 bell peppers, sliced
- 1 onion, sliced
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 cup broccoli florets
- 3 tbsp soy sauce
- 2 tbsp oyster sauce
- 1 tbsp cornstarch
- 1 tsp sesame oil

## Instructions
1. Mix soy sauce, oyster sauce, cornstarch, and sesame oil in a small bowl
2. Heat oil in a wok or large skillet over high heat
3. Add chicken and cook until browned, about 5 minutes
4. Remove chicken and set aside
5. Add vegetables and garlic, stir-fry for 3-4 minutes
6. Return chicken to pan
7. Pour sauce over and toss everything together
8. Cook for 2 more minutes until sauce thickens
9. Serve over rice

**Total Time:** 25 minutes  
**Serves:** 4 people
