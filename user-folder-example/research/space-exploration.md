# Space Exploration Notes

## Current Missions (2026)

### Mars Exploration
- **Perseverance Rover:** Collecting samples for future return mission
- **Ingenuity Helicopter:** Completed extended mission, pioneered Mars aerial exploration
- **Chinese Mars Mission:** Tianwen-1 continuing surface operations
- **Sample Return Mission:** In planning stages (NASA/ESA collaboration)

### Moon Programs
- **Artemis Program:** Establishing lunar base infrastructure
- **Commercial Lunar Payload Services:** Multiple private landers
- **International Lunar Research Station:** China-Russia collaboration
- **Water ice deposits:** Key resource for future bases

### Deep Space Missions
- **James Webb Space Telescope:** Revolutionary discoveries in deep space
- **Europa Clipper:** En route to Jupiter's moon
- **Dragonfly:** Preparing for Titan mission
- **Psyche:** Asteroid belt exploration
