# Sustainable Energy Research

## Overview
Research notes on sustainable energy technologies and their implementation.

## Solar Energy

### Current Technology
- Silicon photovoltaic cells (20-25% efficiency)
- Thin-film solar panels
- Concentrated solar power systems
- Solar thermal collectors

### Emerging Technologies
- Perovskite solar cells (30%+ efficiency potential)
- Organic photovoltaics
- Solar paint and windows
- Space-based solar power

### Key Challenges
- Energy storage and grid integration
- Manufacturing costs and scalability
- Material availability and recycling
- Weather dependency and intermittency

## Wind Energy

### Types
- Onshore wind farms
- Offshore wind turbines
- Vertical axis wind turbines
- Airborne wind energy systems

### Advantages
- Clean and renewable
- Increasingly cost-competitive
- Mature technology
- Creates jobs

### Limitations
- Visual and noise impact
- Bird and bat mortality
- Geographic constraints
- Storage challenges

## Energy Storage

### Battery Technologies
- Lithium-ion (current standard)
- Solid-state batteries
- Flow batteries
- Sodium-ion alternatives

### Other Storage Methods
- Pumped hydro storage
- Compressed air energy storage
- Hydrogen fuel cells
- Thermal energy storage

## Grid Modernization
- Smart grid technologies
- Demand response systems
- Distributed energy resources
- Microgrids and islanding

## Policy Considerations
- Carbon pricing mechanisms
- Renewable energy incentives
- Grid interconnection standards
- International cooperation

## Future Research Directions
- Next-generation materials
- AI-optimized energy systems
- Fusion energy development
- Bioenergy with carbon capture
